Frequently Asked Questions

We've gathered some of the most common questions to help you get started quickly and find the information you need.

What software do I need for the courses?

Our courses primarily use industry-standard free software like Blender. We'll provide links to download any necessary programs, so you don't have to worry about additional costs.

How do I purchase and download 3D models?

After selecting a model, you can add it to your cart and complete your purchase securely. The download link for the model file will be immediately available in your account dashboard and sent to your email address.

What materials can you 3D print with?

We support a wide range of materials, including PLA, ABS, PETG, and resins. When you submit your 3D model for a quote, you can specify your preferred material, and we'll provide an accurate price estimate.

Do you offer a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our courses. For 3D models and printing services, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please refer to our full terms and conditions for details.
